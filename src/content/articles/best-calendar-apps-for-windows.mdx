- Do you like how it looks? You might be able to change the background color,
  the overall theme, the fonts and other interface elements â€” or not able to do
  it. Different apps cater to different segments of the audience, that's why
  some of them feature oldschool designs â€” while others boast modern
  minimalistic ones.

  letting their users add and edit events. Others offer rich and diverse
  features, such as alarms, synchronization with third-party apps, the
  opportunity to write text with an e-pen or dictate it with your voice and so

- Will you be able to download the app on your smartphone or tablet? Some
  solutions have mobile versions while others lack them.
