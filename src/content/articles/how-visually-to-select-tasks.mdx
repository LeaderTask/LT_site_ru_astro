When you have too much tasks, there is desire to visually select some of them.

LeaderTask allows to attach tags to tasks and to paint tasks in the necessary
color. Tags are color "stickers" to the task. Color of the task is meant a color
of a line in the list and also color of a rectangle in the daily log.

Watch two short videos how visually to select tasks.
