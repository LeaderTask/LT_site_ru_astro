Connect the bot from LeaderTask to receive all important notifications in
Telegram. Stay up-to-date on all events on current projects and tasks.

Use our Telegram bot to create tasks using voice commands, define the executor
and the desired due date right in messenger.

\***\*The bot will let you know:\*\***

- about the new task at hand

- chat messages

- status changes and task completion
