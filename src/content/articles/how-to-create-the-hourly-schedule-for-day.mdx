By means of LeaderTask you will be able to create the schedule for a day. And
it's not important how many time your affairs occupy. It can be large affairs
lasting several hours or small-sized tasks for 10 minutes.

Let's open the daily planner:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_1.webp)

Here is it:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_2.webp)

Double click and enter the name of task:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_3.webp)

Done!

In the same way we fill up the remained time:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_4.webp)

Tasks can be moved for other time: just drag them by the left mouse button.

If to click the task, then small squares on top and below will appear. Swipe
them and change business duration:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_5.webp)

If you have a set of small-sized cases, you can bring closer a time line and
each cell will represent not half an hour, but several minutes any more.
Right-click on a column with time and select the necessary scale:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_6.webp)

Here is how the schedule will look if to select cell volume 15 minutes:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_7.webp)

At the increased scale it's possible to fill out the schedule with more
small-sized tasks.

You can change also schedule panel width. For this purpose pick up the left
boundary and move it to the left or to the right:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_8.webp)

For convenience of planning of week we made a possibility to display directly
several days. Select "Three days" for example. They will be displayed in the
form of columns:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_9.webp)

To switch for other days, press the buttons with arrows. Buttons are in the
upper part of a panel:

![](/step1_11.webp)

If you click on the arrow specifying to the right, then next day will be
displayed:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_12.webp)

To change the task, right-click on it. In the shortcut menu all available
operations will be displayed. You can rename a task, delete it, color it and
many other things:

![How to create the hourly schedule for day](/step1_13.webp)

We hope, you easely will make the hourly schedule by means of our scheduler!

You can do it!
