If you have employees with assigned by you tasks, then quite often there can be
such situation: the employee performed the task, but forgot about **some
important trifle**.

People are not robots. It's difficult to them to retain all tiny nuances in the
head.

It is possible to overcome such forgetfulness in trifles!

## Assign the task to the employee in LeaderTask

In title of the task specify an assignment essence

![How to assign tasks that employees couldn't forget about any important small-sized task](/step9_1-1-1024x466.webp)

## Break the task into the main steps

Try to formulate steps as it is possible more simply and more clear. It's the
first step in fight against a forgetfulness

![How to assign tasks that employees couldn't forget about any important small-sized task](/step9_2.webp)

## Write all details in the note of the task

List all important trifles: nuances of execution, possible problems,
requirements to result and other

![How to assign tasks that employees couldn't forget about any important small-sized task](/step9_3.webp)

## Attach documents, photos and any other files to the task

The more evident materials, the probability is less that the performer will
forget about any trifle

![How to assign tasks that employees couldn't forget about any important small-sized task](/step9_4-1.webp)

## If you want to add something to an assignment – write to the built-in chat

In each task the chat is built in. There a manager and the performer can talk to
each other

![How to assign tasks that employees couldn't forget about any important small-sized task](/step9_5.webp)

## Now the employee will not forget about any important trifle
