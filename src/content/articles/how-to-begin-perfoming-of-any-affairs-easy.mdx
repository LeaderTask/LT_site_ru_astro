The most difficult in any business is to begin.

You regularly postpone some business. You promise yourself that you will be
engaged in it since Monday. You justify youselves the fact that there are more
urgent tasks.

You do not take the first step. Business does not move a little. The conscience
begins is interested to look in your side.

LeaderTask helps to begin execution of any business easily. Without tortures and
efforts!

Secret — in magic technology of microactions.

## Technology of microactions

The essence of technology is that any business can be broken into more
small-sized tasks. We call them microactions. Such parts of large business are
executed for very short time: from several minutes till an hour. The plan for
day is formed from several microactions.
