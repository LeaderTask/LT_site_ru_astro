## Factors to Take into Account When Comparing Desktop Reminder Apps

When selecting a reminder app for PC, it's necessary to keep in mind these four
factors:

- Interface. The more user-friendly it is, the better. Some people prefer clean
  minimalistic layouts while others appreciate the chance to customize the app's
  design.

- Features. The app can offer simple one-off or recurring alarms in a pop-up
  window. Alternatively, it can be packed with extra functions and double as a
  full-fledged business or private calendar.

- Cross-platform compatibility. Selected apps allow you to install them only on
  a PC. Others can be compatible with computers or laptops with other operating
  systems as well as Android or iOS smartphones. If the app allows you to sync
  the data across multiple devices, you'll be able to access your reminders even
  from a friend's gadget after they let you log in to your account.

- Price. All solutions from our list are available for free download. Some have
  paid plans too â€” the more extensive the additional functionality, the higher
  the price. In most cases, it's not necessary to upgrade to a paid plan to
  benefit only from the reminders.

We'll start with the most powerful and versatile apps. They're suitable for
planning your work schedule, assigning tasks to others, monitoring your team's
productivity and so on. At the end of the list, we'll switch to niche solutions
that have only one mission: to remind you about important tasks or even a
specific type of task.
