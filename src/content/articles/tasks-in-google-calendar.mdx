If you are used to keeping all your notes, important events and tasks in Google
Calendar or you need an additional reminder to your email - use the task
transfer from LeaderTask! In our [web version](https://app.leadertask.ru/) of
LeaderTask you can now add tasks and reminders directly to Google Calendar.

## How to add a task to Google Calendar

To add a task to your Google calendar using LeaderTask:

1. Navigate to the desired LeaderTask list

2. Add a new task or use a ready-made one, and click on the three dots on the
   right edge of the task

![Add a new task](/task_settings_ltweb.webp)

3. Select "Add to Google Calendar" from the drop-down list box

![Add to Google Calendar](/vipad_spisok-1024x589.webp)

4. The panel for creating a reminder in Google Calendar with the task data from
   LeaderTask will open in a new tab. Make the necessary changes and save the
   task.

![Creating a Google calendar task](/make_task_googlecalendar-1024x788.webp)

5. Done! Task added to Google Calendar

![Google Calendar](/gglcalendar_task-1024x622.webp)
